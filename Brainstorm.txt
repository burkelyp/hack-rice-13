Discord Bot:
1. Bot that binds keyboard to soundtrack function in Discord and can do voice effects
2. Songs that are posted in channel get added to spotify playlist or youtube playlist
3. Events can be added from discord to a google calendar
4. Converts voice to test from voice channel
5. The bot will compile all of the pictures and videos posted in a channel within a certain time frame and create a compilation.
    a. The bot can also organize media by number of interactions/reactions within the channel (if a picture got a lot of reactions, then it was really
       popular and can be included in the list.
    b. Inspiration: "Make a highlight reel from all your time with friends"
